About ACM-W

ACM-W stands for Association for Computing Machinery's Council on Women in Computing. We are a global organization that supports, celebrates, and advocates internationally for the full engagement of women in all aspects of the computing field.

Our local chapter focuses on fostering a vibrant community, providing educational resources, career development opportunities, and promoting diversity and inclusion within the tech industry. We strive to be a supportive hub for all women in computing.

Become a Member

Join ACM-W today and unlock a world of opportunities! Membership offers exclusive access to events, specialized workshops, our mentorship programs, and a supportive community of like-minded individuals.
